Our story
About Pixie.cam 💕

Pixie.cam is a Cebu-based digital camera rental service made for people who want their memories to feel softer, sweeter, and a little more nostalgic. We started with a simple idea: not everyone needs to buy a camera just to enjoy that dreamy point-and-shoot look.

Our pink Kodak cameras are meant for the moments that deserve more than a phone snap — cafe dates, beach trips, birthdays, concerts, barkada hangs, and quiet golden-hour walks. Each rental comes ready to use, beginner-friendly, and styled to fit the cute Pixie.cam vibe.

We keep the process simple and affordable so you can focus on the fun part: taking photos, passing the camera around, and coming home with pictures that feel like keepsakes. 🌸

How does the rental process work? +
Browse our cameras, pick your dates, fill out the booking form, and wait for confirmation. Once confirmed, coordinate pickup. Return the camera on your agreed return date!

Accidents happen! If there is significant damage, we'll discuss fair repair or replacement options. We focus on a friendly and flexible rental experience.
