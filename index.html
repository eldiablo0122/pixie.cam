FAQ
Common questions 🤔
How does the rental process work? +
Browse our cameras, pick your dates, fill out the booking form, and wait for confirmation. Once confirmed, coordinate pickup. Return the camera on your agreed return date!
What if the camera gets damaged? +
Accidents happen! The ₱500 security deposit covers minor wear. For significant damages, repairs will be discussed and billed accordingly. We handle it with understanding!
Is a deposit required? +
Yes, a ₱500 refundable security deposit is required. This is returned in full when the camera is returned safely and in good condition.
How do pickups and returns work? +
We coordinate pickup and return locations upon booking confirmation. We're based in Iloilo City — details will be shared once your booking is confirmed!
